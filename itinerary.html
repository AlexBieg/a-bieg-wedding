Wedding Day Itinerary

September, 2nd

5:00 PM
Arrive at Canlis
Canlis opens at 5, so you may need to wait if you arrive earlier. Canlis does offer valet parking, but if you are planning on drinking we would encourage you to to use a rideshare service or taxi. The Ceremony and Reception will be held up the stairs to the right when you enter.
5:00 PM - 6:30 PM
Photos & Drinks
We'll be doing photos with everyone during this time. The bar will be open for beer and wine.
6:30 PM - 7:00 PM
Ceremony
We get married!
7:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Light Bites & Cocktail Hour
After the ceremony a selection of small bites will be served. Additionally, the bar will be serving cocktails during this hour.
7:30 PM - 8:00 PM
Speeches
Once people have settled a bit, we will set aside some time for anyone who feels inclined to give a toast.
8:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Dinner
Food is a big part of our lives so we hope you enjoy everything. Dinner will start with a Sweet Onion Soubise. You will have your choice of Duck Confit, Wild Salmon, or Forest Mushrooms for the main dish. And desert will be an Apple Cake.
10:00 PM - 12:00 AM
Reception
After dinner we are planning on hanging out and talking with people until Canlis closes at Midnight. We reconginize this is late, and everyone should feel free to head out if they are tired. Wine and beer will be available during this time.
